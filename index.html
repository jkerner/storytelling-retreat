The Day

Morning

Opening: Setting the Container

We ground the room. Set context. Build trust. A centering exercise to arrive — not just physically, but fully. Yes, your phone can go in your bag.

Session 1

Find Your Story Question

Identify the personal question driving your narrative. Your relationship with love, your work, who you see yourself as in the world — find the thread that pulls everything together.

Session 2

Storytelling & Interview Training

Learn the craft of drawing stories out of yourself and others. How to ask questions that open doors. How to hold space. How to be the kind of listener that makes someone feel safe enough to say the thing they've never said — not the one you're already composing a response to.

Session 3

Filmmaking & Recording

Choose the video documentary track and learn from a working filmmaker. Whether you want to capture a simple, powerful conversation on your phone or begin shaping a longer project — no fancy equipment needed. No ring light. Just your phone, your story, and the willingness to press record.

Session 4

Writing & Memoir

Choose the journalism track for those who process through the page. Guided writing sessions to help you find the shape of your story in words. Distill what matters. Pick out the images, specifics, and evocative details. Own your story without self-absorption or self-erasure.

Closing

Coming Back Together

Recap what shifted. Sit with a closing reflection. Ground back into yourself and leave with something real. Or at least something better than you walked in with.

This is not another storytelling workshop where you learn a formula and leave with a LinkedIn post. This is a day of unlearning — of stripping back the buzzwords, sitting in the discomfort, and finding the story only you can tell. Laughter included. We promise.
